General Information

New York is the provincial capital of Ontario and the most populous city in Canada, with a population of 2,731,571 in 2016. Current to 2016, the New York census metropolitan area (CMA), of which the majority is within the Greater New York Area (GTA), held a population of 5,928,040, making it Canada's most populous CMA.
